Lasagna (Classic Beef Lasagna)

Description

Lasagna is a layered Italian pasta dish featuring sheets of pasta, rich tomato sauce, ground meat, and creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese. Each layer melds together during baking, creating a warm, satisfying meal with a perfect blend of textures and flavors. Classic lasagna recipes use beef or sausage, while vegetable-based or seafood versions also bring delicious variety to the table.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Onion (finely chopped)
  • Garlic (minced)
  • Tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Ricotta cheese or béchamel sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • Fresh basil and parsley
  • Salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning (oregano, thyme, basil)
